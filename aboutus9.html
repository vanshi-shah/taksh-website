Our Journey

From Navkar to Taksh Enterprise

Our story began in 1999 when we started as Navkar, with a vision to make quality IT hardware accessible.

In 2015, we evolved and rebranded as Taksh Enterprise, marking a new chapter of growth.

Today, with over 25 years of experience, we serve retail customers, corporates, institutions, and professionals.
